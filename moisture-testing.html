Moisture Testing Services

Protect Your Home from Water Damage

Moisture is the single greatest threat to the structural integrity of a home. Undetected leaks or high humidity levels can lead to rot, structural failure, and hazardous mold growth. Professional moisture testing helps identify problems before they become catastrophic and expensive.

How We Test for Moisture

We use a combination of advanced technology and visual inspection techniques to find moisture where it doesn't belong:

  • Moisture Meters: Digital tools that measure the moisture content in wood, drywall, and other building materials.
  • Thermal Imaging: Infrared cameras can detect temperature differences caused by evaporation, revealing hidden leaks behind walls and ceilings without damage.
  • Hygrometers: Measuring the relative humidity in crawl spaces and basements to identify conditions ripe for mold growth.

Common Problem Areas

Water often enters a home in predictable places, but its effects can be well-hidden:

  • Windows & Doors: Failed flashing or caulking can allow water to seep into the wall framing.
  • Basements & Crawl Spaces: Groundwater intrusion or high humidity can cause floor joist rot and mold.
  • Bathrooms & Kitchens: Slow leaks under sinks or around tubs can destroy subflooring over time.
  • Attics: Roof leaks or poor ventilation can lead to "ice dams" and moisture buildup in insulation.

Why It Matters

Identifying moisture early can save you thousands in remediation costs. More importantly, it ensures your indoor air quality remains healthy for you and your family. If you suspect a leak or have noticed a musty smell, it's time for a professional evaluation.
