Pool Inspection Services

Don't Dive Into Trouble

A swimming pool can be a fantastic addition to any home, providing a place to relax, exercise, and entertain. However, a poorly maintained or damaged pool can quickly become a significant financial burden and a safety hazard. Standard home inspections typically do not include a comprehensive evaluation of the pool and its associated equipment.

Why You Need a Professional Pool Inspection

Whether you're buying a home with an existing pool or just want to ensure your own pool is operating safely and efficiently, a dedicated pool inspection provides peace of mind.

  • Identify Costly Repairs: Pool equipment, such as pumps, filters, and heaters, can be expensive to replace. Discovering issues early can save you thousands.
  • Safety First: We check for proper safety barriers, electrical hazards, and other conditions that could pose a risk to your family and guests.
  • Understand the System: Learn about the condition and operation of the pool's systems so you can make informed maintenance decisions.

What We Inspect

Our comprehensive pool inspection covers all major components of the pool and its environment:

  • Pool Interior & Decking: Checking for cracks, deteriorating plaster or liners, and tripping hazards on the surrounding deck.
  • Equipment Pad: Inspecting the pump, filter, heater, and visible plumbing for leaks, corrosion, and proper operation.
  • Electrical Components: Ensuring lights and pumps are properly grounded and protected by GFCI breakers to prevent shock hazards.
  • Safety Features: Verifying the presence and functionality of gates, fences, and covers in accordance with local safety standards.
  • Accessories: Examining diving boards, slides, and ladders for secure installation and signs of wear.
