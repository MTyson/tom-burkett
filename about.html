About Thomas Ray Burkett Jr

The Eye That Looks Beyond

Thomas Ray Burkett Jr, better known as Tom, is the founder and lead inspector of NOCO Home Inspection Detective. With a commitment to professionalism and a methodical approach to every inspection, Tom has built a reputation as northern Colorado's most thorough home inspector.

Tom's philosophy is simple: he treats every home as if he were buying it himself. This "detective" mindset means looking beyond the surface to find the hidden issues that could impact your investment and your family's safety.

Experience & Expertise

Tom brings a wealth of knowledge to both residential and commercial inspections. He is particularly recognized for his specialized expertise in:

  • Mobile & Manufactured Homes: Understanding the unique structural and maintenance needs of mobile homes.
  • Advanced Testing: Certified in radon testing, sewer scopes, and moisture/mold detection.
  • Methodical Reporting: Providing detailed, easy-to-read reports that empower buyers and sellers with facts.

Certified & Guaranteed

As a member of InterNACHI (International Association of Certified Home Inspectors), Tom adheres to the highest standards of practice and code of ethics in the industry. His work is backed by the $10,000 Honor Guarantee, giving you ultimate peace of mind during one of life's biggest transitions.
